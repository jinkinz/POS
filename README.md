# POS

Full-suite point-of-sale system for food & beverage businesses in Malaysia and Singapore.

Core pillars:

- **POS & payments** — dine-in/takeaway/delivery, MY/SG cards, e-wallets & QR payments, offline-first
- **QR table ordering** — customers scan, order, and pay from their phone; kitchen receives instantly
- **Kitchen display** — station routing, timers, sold-out sync
- **Inventory** — recipes/ingredient deduction, purchasing, consignment, retail stock
- **CRM & loyalty** — members, points, vouchers, campaigns
- **HR & payroll** — rostering, attendance, MY/SG statutory payroll (EPF/SOCSO/PCB, CPF)
- **Invoicing & compliance** — SST/GST, LHDN MyInvois, InvoiceNow (Peppol)
- **Reporting** — sales analytics, menu engineering, accounting exports
